{{'SETUP_WIDGET_ON_WORDPRESS' | translate}}
  • Login to WordPress admin and navigate to the Plugins menu. It’s on the left side of the WordPress dashboard. Then Select Add New.
  • On the Add Plugins page, search for “ngDesk”
  • Select ngDesk from the results and click the Install Now button.
  • You are now in a list of installed plugins. Find the ngDesk plugin in the list of plugins and click on Settings.
    NOTE: you could also access this page by selecting settings->ngDesk from your dashboard menu.
  • Enter the below highlighted subdomain and widgetId under settings and click on “Save”
  • The Widget will now appear on all pages of your WordPress site!
{{'SETUP_WIDGET_ON_JOOMLA' | translate}}
  • Download “ngdesk.zip” from GitHub and unzip it
  • In the Joomla administration panel, go to: Extensions –> Manage –> Install
  • Select “Upload & Install Joomla Extension”, click “Choose file” and select the “ngdesk.zip” file
  • Then click Upload & Install to upload the file and complete the installation
  • For the plugin settings, in the Joomla administration panel go to: Extensions –> Plugin and search for “ngDesk”
  • Enter the below highlighted subdomain and widgetId under settings and click on “Save”
  • The Widget will now appear on all pages of your Joomla site!
{{'SETUP_WIDGET_ON_SHOPIFY' | translate}}
  • Login to your Shopify dashboard
  • Under Apps, click on Visit the Shopify App Store and search for “ngDesk”
  • Select ngDesk from the results, Click on Add app and then click the Install app button.
  • Enter the below highlighted subdomain and widgetId under app and click on “Save”
  • The Widget will now appear on all pages of your Shopify site!
